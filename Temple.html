Sri aadishakthi nagadevi aalayam page is a religious and service motive organization The Temple of Sri Sri Sri Asdishakti nagadevi Aalayam was originally established about 18 years back by Sri Y.Ramana Raju and Smt. Y.Swathi on the devine message of all powerful Goddess Aadisaktinagadevi. In the year of 2007 by the order of goddess Jaganmatha Sri Durga Devi , with the shined shape of “PANCHALOHA STATUE” was inaugurated.

On that day onwards goddess Durga had been glowing with the lighting of crores of suns, with golden colour. Since that day onwards devotees praised,worshipping and visiting her daily. The great sevas are performed in temple to ammavallu like Sahasrakumkumarchana, Kumkuma Poojas ,Astotharakumkumarchana, Panchamrutha Abhishekalu and Ksheerabhishekalu and Chandi Homam (on Moola Nakshathram in every month)

On Pournami of every month is very auspicious day to the Sri Aadisakthi Nagadevi Ammavallu. “Panchamrutha Abhishekalu”, “Abyanganasanalu” with 1008 water pots and “abhishekalu”On Shasti Panchamrutha Abhishekalu and Ksheerabhishekalu are performed to “Sri Subramanyeswara Swamy” . are being performed to the Goddess.
